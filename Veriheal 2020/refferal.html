Veriheal's Referral Program

Know some people that could greatly benefit from having their medical cannabis card? Simply refer them and get rewarded for doing so.

Help your friends find the healing they deserve
  • Earn credits towards any appointment at Veriheal by referring your friends and family.
  • If you refer enough people, you could effectively get your medical marijuana consultation for free!
How it works
  • 1. Anyone with an existing Veriheal patient account can simply login to their profile and access their unique referral code and link.
  • 2. Share your custom referral link and/or coupon code with your friends and family.
  • 3. Once your referrals are approved by a doctor for their medical cannabis card, you receive $5 in Veriheal credit (per referral)
  • 4. Use your referral credits when booking your own appointment through Veriheal!
FAQs

How much money do I get credited per referral?

For each patient that uses your link or code to book a medical cannabis card appointment on the Veriheal platform, who is ultimately approved by a doctor, you receive $5 of credit.

How do I use my Veriheal referral credits?

Once you have successfully earned credits, you can use them upon checkout when booking an appointment with Veriheal. Gaining the right amount of credits could effectively earn you a free Veriheal appointment!

Where do I share my custom codes?

Anywhere you’d like! Many patients choose to share these on their Facebook and Twitter profiles. Others may choose to text or email it to friends and family directly.

How much of a discount are my codes with?

Anyone that uses your link or coupon code will get $5 off an mmj card appointment that they book on Veriheal.

Do I need to have already booked an appointment or been approved by a doctor to start referring my friends and family?

You do not! As soon as you sign up and create an account with Veriheal, you can immediately access your unique referral code and link and start referring people!
