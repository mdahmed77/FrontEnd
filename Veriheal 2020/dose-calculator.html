FAQ's

Can my patients register online so that I will have my information before they arrive to my office or before I see them online?

Yes. This is Veriheal’s main functionality. By the time of your consultation, you will have aa complete patient profile available with all medical records and documents attached to it.

What are the main features of the Veriheal platform?

Veriheal software offers medical cannabis physicians a secure, HIPAA compliant, cloud platform for managing their patients, patient files, appointments and patient appointment notifications.

Are there any hidden costs?

There are no cost to join the physician’s network of Veriheal. Everything is included in our team membership and we welcome new physicians into our network every week.
