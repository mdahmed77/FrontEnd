Veriheal's Innovation in Cannabis Scholarship

To further the push for cannabis studies, we are offering up to 10 $1,000 scholarships to students that prove efficacy towards cannabis studies, research, business, economics, and innovation as a whole.

About the Scholarship

At Veriheal, we value education deeply and want to help students in need. We recognize that any higher education can be a very significant expense. In addition to ever-rising tuition, students may be responsible to pay for housing and other school-related expenses. Attending university can effectively cost students hundreds of thousands of dollars. Veriheal is proud to offer some financial help to students and their families in the form of $1,000 scholarships to individuals who are currently attending university in the United States.

Winners will be selected from students who show the best combination of sustainability, community, awareness, and innovation for the future of cannabis not only as an industry but as a medical medium.

Who is this for?
  • Business student who sees the potential for a product that could revolutionize the way we purchase cannabis,
  • Computer science student who wants to develop a more efficient cannabis delivery app than what is on the market.
  • Student in nursing school who is considering the application of cannabis-based medicines to patients suffering with a pains that traditional forms of medicine are not helping or have nasty side effects.

Everyone is welcome to apply.

The top applications will win $1,000 each!

Application Requiremnts
  • Essay on how to improve the cannabis industry. Think outside of the box. Minimum of 1,000 words. Consider your idea, its implementation, and how it can help the industry.
  • Only applications from .edu email address will be considered
  • Scholarship materials must be submitted by July 30, 2020
  • Depending on the number of submissions, scholarship winners will be selected within the weeks following the submission deadline.
  • Please submit the requested information using the form below.
